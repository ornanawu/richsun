Automatic Rotated Pour Over Coffee Maker

Features

Double wall water tank

350ml transparent heat resistant double wall water tank keeps water hot for longer.

SAutomatic 360º pour over

The water tank is 360º rotating to pour the hot water to the ground coffee.

Extract Coffee Evenly

Uses moving plural streams of water to agitates the coffee grounds and is poured in circle to evenly saturate the coffee grounds.

Simple Operation

Simple retro style on/off switch.

Different Types of Carafes to Choose

  • A. Regular Carafe
  • B. Double Wall Glass Carafe:allows for longer heat retention

    • Instruction

    1. Place a filter in the dripper

    We recommend wetting the filter with hot water and then dumping the water before proceeding with brewing.

    2. Add the ground coffee

    Add 20g ground coffee to the filter and gently tap it to level the surface of the grounds.

    3. Place the dripper to the unit

    Make sure the Dripper slip into the hole of dripper plate.

    4. Power On

    Switch Power to on position.

    5. Water Tank Start to Rotate

    The water tank is 360º rotating.

    6. Pour hot water

    Pour 300-350ml of hot water to water tank. Water Tank Capacity Max : 350ml.

    7.Place Cover of Water Tank

    8.Brewing time

    Brewing time around 4 minutes.

    9.Enjoy!
