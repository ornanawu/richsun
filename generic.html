360º Rotated Pour Over System

“Unlike other drip coffee makers, Oceanrich's 360º Rotated Pour Over Brewing System delivers precise amounts of hot water from its clear rotating water tank. This is done through multiple streams, allowing just the right amount of water and time for the coffee grounds to absorb and extract the coffee’s true rich and full-bodied flavor.

The secret is in the way the hot water agitates and saturates the coffee grounds as the motor rotates the water tank for few minuets. The 360º Rotated Pour Over Brewing System uses a clear BPA free Tritan water tank that holds enough water, to make cups of the richest, most robust aroma coffee that you have ever had."
