Grine & Brew Coffee Maker

Features

Quick & Easy to Grind Brew Fresh Coffee

With built-in grinder system elevates the single cup brewing experience to a bolder, more satisfying taste.Just add whole beans and enjoy fresh coffee flavor each time you brew a cup.

Stainless Steel Permanent Filter

Stainless Steel permanent filter does not absorb essentials oils from coffee beans, allowing you to make a perfectly clear and flavorful coffee every single time.

Flexible

Adjustable coffee outlet to accommodate large mugs or short cups for splash-free brewing.

Compact Size

It has a compact design that can fit perfectly on a small kitchen space.It is even easy to store in cabinets as it comes small..

Instruction

STEP1
Add Coffee Bean

Add the desired amount of coffee into the 2-in-1 Grinder and Permanent Filter.

STEP2
Fill in Water

Pour the the desired amount of fresh, cold water into the water reservoir. Do not fill with more than 200ml of water.

STEP3
Press the Power Button

(a) If using whole beans Press the upper button once to grind and brew automatically. (b) If using pre-ground coffee Press the bottom button once to begin the brew cycle.
